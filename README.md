# Contractor Executive Dashboard
Bridges the gap between project management in Knowify and financials in Quickbooks by integrating data to provide real-time insight into important KPIs that help you make decisions about your company easily, without having to manually fetch or find data. A significant problem in contractor platforms is their narrow scopes on project management or financial, making it impossible to have everything united in one place. This project creates a data pipeline to unite this data and calculate aggregate statistics as needed in one place. You can use AI to assist in analyzing your specific data by creating charts or giving you areas to look into.
